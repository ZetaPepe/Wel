ATM Innovative Technology

ATM, as an innovative digital asset issued based on BSC (Binance Smart Chain), aims to solve the pain points in the traditional cryptocurrency field through a series of cutting-edge technologies, and provide investors and users with more stable, fair and efficient digital financial services. The following is a detailed introduction to the four major innovative technologies adopted by ATM:

1. No increase in circulation

ATM adopts a unique total amount locking mechanism to ensure that its circulation will never increase after issuance. This design avoids the market supply and demand imbalance and currency depreciation caused by continuous issuance. Investors can hold ATM with confidence without worrying about its value dilution due to issuance. By controlling the circulation, ATM can maintain the relative stability of the market and provide investors with a more reliable means of value storage.

2. No weight dilution

ATM's weight distribution mechanism is also carefully designed to ensure that the rights and interests of early participants and long-term holders are fully protected. Over time, ATM's weight distribution will not be diluted due to the addition of new users or the inflow of new funds. This means that whether it is early investors or long-term holders, the ATMs in their hands will maintain relatively stable weights and influence, so as to share the growth dividends of ATM more fairly.

3. U pool (stablecoin reserve pool)

ATM introduces the concept of U pool (stablecoin reserve pool) to further enhance its price stability and risk resistance. The U pool consists of a large number of stablecoins (such as BUSD, etc.) as a solid backing for the ATM price. When the market fluctuates violently, the U pool will play a buffering role and maintain its price stability by buying or selling ATM. This mechanism not only helps to reduce market panic, but also provides investors with a relatively safe value haven.

4. Normal fluctuations in currency prices

Although ATM has taken many measures to maintain its price stability, as a freely traded digital asset, its price may still fluctuate normally due to various factors such as market sentiment, supply and demand. The ATM team believes that moderate price fluctuations are a reflection of market vitality and an opportunity for investors to trade and arbitrage. Therefore, ATM encourages investors to view price fluctuations rationally and make wise investment decisions based on a full understanding of market conditions and risks.
